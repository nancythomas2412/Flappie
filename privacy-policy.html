Quick Summary: Flappie respects your privacy. We collect minimal data to enhance your gaming experience and show relevant ads. Game progress is stored locally on your device. We use Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect advertising data as described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data (Stored Locally)

Flappie stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Your high scores, games played, and achievement progress
  • Game Statistics: Play time, pipes passed, coins collected, power-ups used
  • Settings: Sound/music preferences, difficulty settings, tutorial completion status
  • Customization: Selected bird hats and unlocked cosmetic items
  • Session Data: Current win streaks, daily play streaks, and last play date
  • Purchase History: In-app purchases and premium upgrades (stored locally)

1.2 Advertising Data (Collected by Google AdMob)

When ads are displayed, Google AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID: Device advertising identifier for personalized ads
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, screen size
  • App Usage: How you interact with ads and the app
  • Location Data: Approximate location for location-based ads (if permitted)
  • Ad Performance: Which ads you view and interact with

Note: This data is collected and processed by Google according to their privacy policy.

1.3 What We DON'T Collect Directly

Flappie (DeCoCo) does NOT directly collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Information from other apps
  • Precise location data (only general location for ads if permitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

The game data we store locally is used exclusively to:

  • Save Your Progress: Remember your high scores and achievements
  • Customize Experience: Apply your preferred settings and customizations
  • Show Statistics: Display your personal gaming statistics
  • Maintain Game State: Resume games and preserve your progress
  • Unlock Content: Track which cosmetic items you've unlocked

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All your game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences system. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through your device's backup settings.

3.2 Data Security

  • Data is stored in your app's private directory, inaccessible to other apps
  • No network transmission of personal or gameplay data
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • We use Android's built-in security mechanisms to protect stored data

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties.

Flappie operates with minimal data sharing only through advertising partners as described above.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Flappie is designed to be safe for players of all ages, including children under 13:

  • We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children
  • No online chat or communication features
  • No social media integration
  • No advertising or third-party content
  • Fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

6. Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View your statistics and progress in the app's Statistics screen
  • Delete: Clear data by using "Reset Statistics" in the app or uninstalling
  • Control: All data stays on your device under your control
  • Portability: Data can be backed up through Android's backup system

7. Permissions

Flappie requests these permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for displaying ads and ad functionality
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Check network connectivity for ads
  • WAKE_LOCK: Prevents screen from turning off during gameplay
  • Storage: Save game progress and settings locally

We do not request permissions for:

  • Precise location services (only general location for ads if permitted)
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Phone or SMS access
  • External storage access

7.1 Advertising Permissions

For advertising functionality, Google AdMob may request:

  • Advertising ID access: For personalized ads (can be reset in device settings)
  • Approximate location: For location-based ads (optional, user-controlled)
  • Ad interaction tracking: To measure ad effectiveness

7.2 Ad Control Options

You can control advertising data collection:

  • Reset Advertising ID: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads > Reset advertising ID
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Premium upgrade: Purchase premium upgrade to remove ads completely
  • Google Ad Settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your ad preferences

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. When we make changes:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
  • Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance

9. International Users

Flappie can be played worldwide. Game data is stored locally on your device, while advertising data may be transferred internationally by Google AdMob as described in their privacy policy. The app complies with major privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing game functionality and saving progress
  • Consent: Your explicit consent when you start playing the game

12. Data Retention

Game data is retained locally on your device until:

  • You manually reset your statistics in the app
  • You uninstall the application
  • You clear the app's data through device settings
